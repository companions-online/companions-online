# Survival basics

Your character has 100 HP, no hunger meter, and a five-second
respawn. You don't have to eat to live, but you do have to survive
the night.

## Health and dying

Damage comes from wolves, skeletons, and the occasional bear. When
your HP hits zero you fall over, drop everything you had equipped
on the ground where you died, and respawn at the world's spawn
point five seconds later with full HP. Your inventory survives —
only the items you had in your hand or armor slots scatter — so
running back to your death tile to pick them up is a normal part
of play.

You heal by eating cooked food (Cooked Meat, Cooked Fish) or by
using a Bandage. There's no passive regeneration.

## Day and night

A full in-world day takes about 14 minutes of real time. Night
runs from 20:00 to 5:00; everything between those is daylight.

- **Daytime** is for gathering, crafting and exploring. The
  monsters that bother you are wolves (5-tile aggro) and bears, both
  of which you'll usually see coming.
- **Night** is when the skeletons come out. They spawn in the
  dark, in unlit ground, and roughly one new skeleton appears per
  in-world hour somewhere within 10–20 tiles of a player. They
  don't despawn at sunrise — instead, sunlight burns them: 4
  damage every second or so until they fall. You can outlast them
  if you have walls and a campfire.

A campfire lights a 6-tile radius around itself, both visually and
for the spawn check — skeletons won't appear inside its glow. Two
or three campfires turn a small base into a no-spawn zone.

## Critters

| Creature | Behavior | Drops |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rabbit | Wanders, flees from you. | — |
| Deer | Wanders, flees within 3 tiles. | 2 Hide, 1 Raw Meat |
| Fox | Wanders, mildly skittish. | 1 Hide |
| Wolf | Wanders, aggros within 5 tiles, attacks. | 2 Hide, 1 Raw Meat |
| Bear | Wanders, aggros, hits hard. | 3 Hide, 2 Raw Meat |
| Skeleton | Night-only spawn, aggros, attacks. | 1–2 Iron, 1 Rock |

Every kill drops items as ground entities — walk over and click to
pick them up.

## Cooking

Raw Meat and Raw Fish on their own won't heal you much; cook them
first.

1. Build a Campfire (Crafting → Placeables) and place it.
2. Equip the raw food to your hand (drag it to a quickslot, or
   click the slot, or press 1–9).
3. Stand next to the campfire and click or tap it. The food
   transforms — Raw Meat becomes Cooked Meat, Raw Fish becomes
   Cooked Fish.

Cooked food restores meaningfully more health than raw, and the
campfire doubles as your skeleton repellent overnight.

## A first-night plan

If you've just spawned and don't know what to do: chop a couple of
trees, grab a few rocks, craft an Axe and a Wooden Club, then
craft a Campfire and place it. As the sky darkens, stay inside the
firelight. Iron from skeleton drops becomes the gateway to better
weapons and armor on day two.
